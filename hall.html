Hall Facilities



Layout type : Stalls

The expo hall can accommodate a total of 41 stalls which each stall measuring 10 ft x 10 ft. A participating company can also take multiple stalls and do the necessary customization.

Layout type: Conference – Theatre Style

The expo hall can accommodate 500 PAX theatre style seating with sufficient space for walkway all around

Layout type : Conference – Round Table Style

The expo hall can accommodate 186 PAX conference style seating with sufficient space for walkway all around



Emergency Exit

The conference hall is well equipped with visible signage for emergency exit. The emergency exit are carefully provided to ensure proper evacuation in situations of emergency.
